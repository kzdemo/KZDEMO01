We defend your ideas with IP enforcement, protection, and strategic counseling.

Koning Zollar specializes in patent infringement, trademark, trade dress, trade secret, and copyright litigation, as well as computer software, cloud, and mobile disputes.

Patent Litigation

Our attorneys are skilled in prosecuting and defending patent litigation matters in U.S. federal district courts across the nation and before the Patent Trial and Appeal Board. We emphasize the early formulation of case strategy and hands-on leadership, from pre-suit considerations through discovery, motion practice, and trial.

Our team has handled cases in major patent jurisdictions, including the Eastern District of Texas; Delaware; the Central and Northern Districts of California; and Virginia. Due to our broad technical backgrounds and wide-ranging industry knowledge, we provide clients — from small start-ups to established corporations — with the versatility and ingenuity to meet their patent litigation needs.

Trade Secret Litigation

We represent both plaintiffs and defendants in federal and state trade secret litigation. We have deep experience in many technology areas and employee trade secret cases. We bring and defend against applications for temporary restraining orders and preliminary injunction orders. We also counsel clients on how best to protect their trade secrets.

Copyright Litigation

We represent clients in a wide array of complex copyright matters, including matters related to software copyrightability. We also counsel clients on how best to protect their copyrightable works.

IP Strategy

The firm works with both start-ups and established companies to strategize and prioritize their IP protection. Whether it's deciding what technology to patent, providing freedom-to-operate or invalidity analyses, or determining whether patents are a better choice over trade secret protection, we work with clients to solve their IP needs effectively and efficiently.
