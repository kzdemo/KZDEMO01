About the Firm

Koning Zollar LLP is a litigation boutique built on a single principle: every client deserves the caliber of representation typically reserved for the largest corporations.

Our founding partners bring decades of experience from top international law firms and bring that expertise to bear for clients of every size — from emerging companies to Fortune 500 organizations.

We operate with the focus of a boutique and the firepower of a major firm. When outcomes matter, our clients trust us.
