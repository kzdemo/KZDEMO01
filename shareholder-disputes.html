We defend companies and their representatives in all types of shareholder disputes.

Our attorneys have deep experience representing corporate defendants in the most complex and challenging securities matters. Our practice runs the gamut of securities law issues under the Securities Act of 1933, the Securities and Exchange Act of 1934, the Commodity Exchange Act, the Investment Companies Act, and state law.

Koning Zollar attorneys have represented clients in a wide variety of securities matters, including securities class actions, shareholder derivative suits, SEC enforcement actions, and related corporate governance disputes.

We bring the same aggressive, client-focused approach to securities matters that defines all of our work — protecting our clients' interests at every stage of the litigation.
