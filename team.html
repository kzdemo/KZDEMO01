Blake Zollar

Partner

A business litigator, Blake helps individuals and companies resolve complex commercial, employment, and shareholder disputes. He has successfully represented clients in breach of contract claims, securities fraud, trade secret matters, government investigations, and wage and hour class actions. Prior to co-founding Koning Zollar, Blake was an attorney at Cooley LLP.
